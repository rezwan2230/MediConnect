Powerful Features

MediConnect offers a comprehensive suite of tools to streamline healthcare management for both patients and providers.

Easy Appointment Booking

Schedule appointments with your preferred doctors in just a few clicks. Receive instant confirmation and reminders.

Digital Prescriptions

Doctors can create and share prescriptions digitally. Patients can access them anytime from their dashboard.

Medical Records

Securely store and manage your complete medical history. Share records with doctors with just one click.

Video Consultations

Connect with doctors remotely through secure video calls. Perfect for follow-ups and minor consultations.

Medication Reminders

Never miss a dose with smart medication reminders. Customizable alerts for your specific regimen.

Health Analytics

Track your health metrics over time. Visual reports help you and your doctor make informed decisions.
